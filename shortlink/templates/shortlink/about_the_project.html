What is our project about?


Just a pet project

First of all YA.LINK have been started as a pet project for training Python/Django skills.

So if you read this, the project succeed.


Unlimited links and domains

From the very beginning of YA.LINK it was planned as a free open source project. In case of URL-shortener it'll always mean

a possibility for a user to own unlimited permanent links and domains for free.


UTM generator

YA.LINK provide a UTM-link generator as a useful additional feature. Now you can generate, shorten and

manage UTM-links for your campaign in one place.


Secure links

This service enables registered users to transfer their sensitive data without the risk of compromising it.

This is achieved through links that have a limited lifetime. Upon reaching the limit value of the counter,

this link and all the data to which it led are completely deleted from the server.
